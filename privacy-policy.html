Privacy Policy

last updated: July 2026

At devnotes we respect your privacy. This page explains, in plain terms, what information is collected when you visit the site, how it's used, and what choices you have.

What we collect

This site doesn't ask you to create an account or collect personal information directly (like your name or email), unless you choose to contact us voluntarily. Like most websites, though, we use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Understand how the site is used (most-visited pages, time spent reading).
  • Show ads, including ads personalized based on your browsing activity.

Advertising & Google AdSense

This site displays ads through Google AdSense (or other ad networks). These third-party providers may use cookies to show you ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites on the internet.

  • Google uses advertising cookies to serve ads relevant to your browsing.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Learn more about how Google uses data from partner sites in Google's "How Google uses information from sites that use our services" page.
your control
You can block or delete cookies anytime through your browser settings. Some site features may work differently without them.

Links to other sites

This site may contain links to other pages or external resources. We're not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites, so we recommend reading their own policies.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the corresponding update date.
